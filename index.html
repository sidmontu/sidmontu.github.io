Welcome to my personal page. Here, I try to keep an up-to-date account of projects I have worked on, and also write blog posts on the side. I have worked intimately with FPGAs in the past, and I believe they will play an increasingly important role in the post-Moore future of computing. Lately, my interests have revolved around the fast-evolving space of deep learning, in particular natural language processing.

Language is a "structured system of communication". Yet, in practice, we struggle to use computers to understand and model this structure effectively, something the human brain does effortlessly. I am interested in building better language models, and applying them to do useful things in a variety of domains.
